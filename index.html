James Webb Space Telescope

The James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) is the most powerful space telescope ever built. It helps us observe the first galaxies that formed after the Big Bang, study exoplanets, and peer into star-forming regions.

Artemis II Update

NASA's Artemis II mission will carry the first crewed spacecraft to the Moon in over 50 years, paving the way for sustainable lunar exploration.

Mars Sample Return

NASA's Perseverance rover continues collecting samples on Mars for the upcoming Mars Sample Return mission, which aims to bring Martian soil to Earth for analysis.
